about androcetus

Sunday, October 19, 2014
Androcetus is a scratchpad of thoughts about topics such as work, success, relationships & life viewed through a lense of masculinity and where possible, femininity. I’ll make the same caveat that David Deida makes which is that we are not talking about the behavior and thougths of the biological sexes of males or females (boy parts, girl parts, or both in some cases). However, we are discussing the Yin and Yang of gender that we call masculine and feminine.
